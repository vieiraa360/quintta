What is Kambo?

Kambo is a secretion produced by the green tree frog Phyllomedusa Bicolor also known as Sapo ,and live in Amazonian Rainforests.
International scientific research has been conducted on the properties of Kambo since the 1980´s. The studies have found that Kambo frog venom is made up of many peptides that are strengthening and healing to the human body and immune system, whilst physically destroying pathogenic microorganisms.
It has strong natural anti-inflammatory, antibiotic, antimicrobial and anaesthetic substances. Kambo contains a complex blend of bioactive peptides, around 139 peptides known to be crucial to the health, good function, growth and repair of our systems.
